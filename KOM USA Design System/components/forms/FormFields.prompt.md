Form primitives for quote-request / contact forms: labeled `Input`, `Select`, and `Checkbox`.

```jsx
<Input label="Full name" placeholder="Jane Homeowner" />
<Select label="Service needed" options={["Roofing", "Remodel", "Repair"]} />
<Checkbox label="I'd like a callback" />
```

Border defaults to Concrete Gray, focus ring switches to Field Green. Square-ish corners (`--radius-sm`) to match brand's practical, non-decorative form style.
