# KOM USA Brand Guide

## Brand Direction

KOM USA should feel friendly, hometown, practical, and professional. The visual system should avoid harsh neon green and heavy all-black layouts. Use warm white space, charcoal typography, soft green accents, and strong project photography.

## Logo

Use `logos/kom-usa-logo-primary-charcoal.svg` as the default logo.

Recommended versions:

- Primary: Charcoal logo on warm white or white
- Reverse: White logo on charcoal or deep green
- Accent: Green logo for limited brand moments

Do not stretch, skew, outline, recolor randomly, or place the logo on low-contrast backgrounds.

## Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---:|---|
| Primary | KOM Sage Green | `#78A866` | Brand accents and soft backgrounds |
| Action | KOM Field Green | `#2F6B3B` | Buttons, links, strong CTAs |
| Core | Graphite Charcoal | `#33383E` | Logo, text, navigation |
| Background | Warm White | `#F7F7F2` | Main page background |
| Neutral | Concrete Gray | `#E6E3DA` | Borders, dividers, forms |
| Secondary | Steel Blue Gray | `#5E7480` | Icons and secondary accents |
| Accent | Brass Gold | `#C59A42` | Small premium highlights |

## Typography

Use Nunito Sans for both headlines and body text.

- H1: 700 weight
- H2/H3: 700 weight
- Body: 400 or 500 weight
- Buttons: 700 weight

## Website Style

- Use simple, clean layouts.
- Lead with real project photos and trust signals.
- Keep buttons clear and practical.
- Prefer square or lightly rounded corners.
- Avoid decorative gradients, oversized shadows, and overly playful graphics.

## CSS Tokens

Use `web/brand-tokens.css` as the starting point for the website.
